Who We Are

We are a group of professionals hoping to inspire the leaders of the future. We offer leadershipand creativity training to help you advance your career.

Our Story

Our inspiration for No Sheep Leadership came from watching many of our fellow peers constantly get rejected from jobs because of their lack of passion. We see this every day, people all around the world simply do the same repetitive tasks, and never unleash their full potential. The difference between a true leader and an average person is a simple skill set. We coach both working professionals and students to break those bad habits and develop a strong leadership skill set.
