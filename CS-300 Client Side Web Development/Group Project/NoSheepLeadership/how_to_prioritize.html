Taking Care of Yourself

Takeing Care of Yourself

Taking care of yourself is one of the most important aspects you can do on becoming a good leader. Every leader needs to care for oneself before they can lead others. The best thing about self-care is that it’s free! No need to empty out your wallets on expensive facials or messages. Also make sure to check out our page on How to Break Bad Habits. Here are ten affordable to no-cost tips for good self-care habits:

Self-Care Tips
  1. Drink water! Every leader needs to drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  2. Every leader needs sufficient amount of sleep and good quality of sleep! Nothing is more important than having a good night’s rest.
  3. Detox from technology! Technology is known to be a stressor due to information overload. Those emails and phone calls can wait!
  4. Exercise! Exercising have been linked to increased productivity and the quality of sleep. There are so many types of workouts to choose from: pilates, yoga, cardio, running, walking, the list goes on.
  5. Mediate! Mediation has been known to decrease stress and improve sleep.
  6. Have a morning and night routine!
  7. Eat nutritious foods! Fruit and veggies have been known to contain vitamins, minerals, fiber, and omega-3s.
  8. Have a gratitude notebook!
  9. Read a book!
  10. 4-7-9 breathing technique has been known to decrease stress. Inhale for four seconds, hold for seven seconds, and exhale for eight seconds.
