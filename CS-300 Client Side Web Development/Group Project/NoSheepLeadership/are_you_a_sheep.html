Are You a Sheep?

Are you a Sheep?
  1. When making important choices, do you?
    A - Research and make the best choice suited for you
    B - Ask for someone else to make it
  2. When listening to music, do you?
    A - Listen to a wide range of music
    B - Listen to only radio music
  3. When picking a book to read, do you?
    A - Read a bit from the book before buying it
    B - Only read a book that is a best seller
  4. When planning a travel trip, do you?
    A - Choose a place that interest you
    B - Choose a place that is popular
  5. Finally, will you take this quiz with a pinch of salt?
    A - Yes
    B - Who would eat salt after a quiz?
