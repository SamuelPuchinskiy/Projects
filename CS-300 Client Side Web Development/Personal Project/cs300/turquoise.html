Turquoise

By far, Turquoise is my favorite color because the color reminds of the sea. Turquoise is a blue-greenish color and the color comes from the crystal, Turquoise. The color is more of a cooler tone, than a warm tone. According to Cyriac, the color blue is known to relax the nervous system and promotes calmness and green is known for nature and to encourage trust. In addition, Turquoise is a color used mainly for tourism sites or sites that have nature related content. Turquoise is a color similar to the ocean, which could be an excellent color for airline or hotel websites. According to RapidTables, the Hex code for Turquoise is #40E0D0 and the RGB code is (64,224,208).
